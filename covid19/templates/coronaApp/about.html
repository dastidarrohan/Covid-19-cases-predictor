About this website

This website is all about visualization and prediction of Covid-19 cases. It lets you forecast the Covid-19 cases that is dormant in the world, such as total number of confirmed cases, deaths, recovery and the rate of all these parameters. This website makes use of the John Hopkins University API to fetch the current status. It also lets you analyse yourself and gives you an estimation of the likelihood of you being infected with the virus.(Discretion: Even though this website makes use of advanced machine learning algorithms to give you the results, still, it cannot replace a real doctor.So don't freak out and seeking the help of a medical expert is strongly advised before contemplating any self-diagnosis.)
